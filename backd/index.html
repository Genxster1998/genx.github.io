Hi. Welcome to Backd

When I created Nitrux® S.A., I wanted it to be a one of a kind company, I wanted it to be the company that helped those developers that spend their free time making, building and maintaining their open-source projects by providing them financial backing where they have none, with Backd® this is exactly what we're going to do.

Uri Herrera - Nitrux S.A Founder



We want to give back to the people who have help us become what we are now. Many developers would like to spend their time working on their projects, but often these projects end up being only hobbies instead of becoming full-time paid jobs. So we thought, "Wouldn't it be great if these people would got rewarded for their efforts?". We want to transform this landscape by providing them financial backing where they have none for it. Because we didn't received the help we would've wanted to but others don't have to go through the same.


We're going to take part of the money we make as a company and offer a fund for them to continue their work on their project, yes, you read that right, and this is where our NXQ® comes into play.


Out of the sales of the NXQ® we'll give money to one open-source project, it doesn't matter whether the project is big or small. For every $4,000 USD that we make out of the sales of our mini-PC we'll give $1,000 USD to a chosen open-source project for them to spend it on further developing it or in other words 25% and the rules are pretty simple:


  • • Projects can only participate one time every year.
  • • They can't participate again until the next year.
  • • Projects that enter should not have any corporate sponsorship already.
  • • Up to 10 projects will be able to participate each round.
  • • Projects must have good documentation.
  • • Projects must have an own site or a web page hosted in GitHub® (example.github.io).
  • • Projects must be hosted at GitHub® and be continuosly developed.
  • • Only the project leader(s)/owner(s) can inscribe the project.
  • • A verified PayPal® account is a must.
  • • Project entries will be reviewed before being accepted into the program.
  • • Software, Hardware and Artwork projects can enter.
    •

If you would like to see your project take off, and receive the funds necessary for you to develop, maintain and further improve it all you have to do is get in touch with us and tell us about your project for you to get Backd®.
